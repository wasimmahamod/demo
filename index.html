How does the club fund work?

The Anti Cash Club fund is a mutual treasury of the club members. The members will be able to vote how the club funds are distriputed. Our goal is to collect NFT bluechips and early projects with great potential. The profits of the club fund can later be shared between the club members. 20% of the ACCC mint sales will go straight to the mutual fund and 70% of the secondary market royalties will be collected to the fund after minting.
